# Pricing Page Design Overrides

> **Extends:** Master Design System

## Page Patterns

### Market & Size Selectors
- **Style:** Pill-style tabs (not standard buttons).
- **Active State:** Background `#00C89615`, border-color `#00C896`, color `#00C896`.
- **Inactive State:** Background `#1A1A1A`, border `#333333`, color `#AAAAAA`.
- **Spacing:** Large gaps (`2rem`+) between account size pills.

### Drawdown Type Selection
- **Layout:** 4 cards in a **2x2 grid**.
- **Interaction:** Hovering a card should apply the Racing Green border glow.

### Comparison Table
- **Header:** `#0A0A0A` background.
- **Icons:** **Circle checkmarks** ONLY (`#00C89615` background, `#00C896` icon).
- **Text:** Price data in `JetBrains Mono`.

## Anti-Patterns
- ❌ Do NOT use plain text checkmarks.
- ❌ Do NOT use standard drop-downs for account sizes.
